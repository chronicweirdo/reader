Chronic Reader Help

Library Page

This page lets you navigate the available collection of books and comics, search, view progress of books you have opened and read. New books are loaded as you scroll. Use the search field to look for available books. Click on a book or comic to start reading it.

Collections Page

Every book and comic is part of a collection. The collections page displays all the collections in the app and can be a faster way of discovering what is available. You can click on a collection to go back to the library page and only display the entries in that collection.

Book/Comic Page

You can use the higlighted areas of the screen to move between pages/views or to open the tools menu.

The tools menu has a left-side and a right-side version, depending on the tools button you pressed. It is designed to be easy to use whatever hand you hold your device in.

You can pinch-zoom or use the mouse wheel to adjust the size of the comic page or the book font size. If you have a keyboard, use arrow keys or W/A/S/D to navigate.

When reading a comic, press left mouse and drag or touch screen and drag to adjust the position of the comic page. A double left click or a double tap on the center of the screen zooms you in or out on the comic page.
