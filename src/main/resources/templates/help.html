Chronic Comic Reader Help

Library Page

This page lets you navigate the available collection of comics, search, view progress of comics you have opened and read. New comics are loaded as you scroll. Use the search field to look for available comics. Click on a comic to start reading it.

Collections Page

Every comic can be part of a collection. The collections page displays all the collections in the app and can be a faster way of discovering what is available. You can click on a collection to go back to the library page and only display the comics in that collection.

Comic Page

You can use the higlighted areas of the screen to move between pages/views or to open the tools menu.

The tools menu has a left-side and a right-side version, depending on the tools button you pressed. It is designed to be easy to use whatever hand you hold your device in.

You can pinch-zoom or use the mouse wheel to adjust the size of the comic page. Press left mouse and drag or touch screen and drag to adjust the position of the comic page. Click on the left and right sides of the screen to move to the previous or next view. A single left click or a single tap in the center of the screen will display the comic tools. A double left click or a double tap on the center of the screen zooms in or out. If you have a keyboard, use arrow keys or W/A/S/D to navigate. Use back button or back gesture to go back to library page.
